1 # LatticeYangMills
2 Suite for generating field configurations in pure Yang-Mills SU(3) theory and compute basic observables on them. By Giovanni Pederiva.
3 
4 See https://github.com/GioPede/MasterThesis for the Master of Science thesis this code has been developed for.
5 
6 ## Current Implementation
7 ### Algorithms
8 - Metropolis Algorith for generating ensembles of lattice gauge field configurations
9 - Gradient Flow see https://arxiv.org/abs/1006.4518 for studying non linear properties of the fields
10 ### Observables
11 - Plaquette
12 - Energy Density (clover)
13 - Topological Charge (clover)
14 ### Actions
15 - Wilson Gluon Action
